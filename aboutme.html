Hello, I'm Surya KP

I'm an Aspiring second year college student pursuing Btech CSE with specialization in AIML at SRMIST, Katankulathur with an aim to change the world through my skills and knowledge.
My fascination and love for these magnificent creatures started at a young age, sparked by encounters with documentaries and wildlife books.
I have always been a lover and an admirer of animals, in order to protect and preserve these absolute beauties of our world, I've created this website.
As I delved deeper into the subject, I became increasingly aware of the alarming rate at which species are disappearing due to human activities such as habitat destruction, poaching, and climate change.
This realization propelled me to take action and contribute to the conservation efforts in any way I could.

SUE - Save US Endangeredspecies

The main goal of this website is to raise awareness when it comes to endangered species.
Motivated by my passion and driven by a sense of responsibility, I decided to create this website as a platform to raise awareness about the plight of endangered species.
In my website, The animals talk in their own perspectives of what has been happening to them and the habitats they live in.
Through captivating imagery, and impactful stories, I aim to shed light on the challenges faced by these animals and the urgent need for conservation action.

My goal is not only to inform but also to inspire others to join the fight to protect our planet's biodiversity.
By fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for endangered species, I believe we can work together to create a future where wildlife thrives alongside humanity.
Join me on this journey to make a difference and safeguard the wonders of our natural world for generations to come.
Together, we can be the voice for the voiceless and ensure a brighter future for all living beings.
